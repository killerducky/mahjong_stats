Mahjong Soul Charts

  • Usage: Input username (and change idx if needed) and click Generate Graph

  • First chart shows expected rank point change per match played.
    • For example, if you average 25% for 1/2/3/4 placements and your current rank is M1, your expected rank point change will be 0.
    • The blue lines show breakeven points for different ranks.
      • For example, if you are M1 and gain 3.75 points per round (15/4), you would be breakeven at M2.
      • Norm Rank controls which rank the graph will assume you are.
      • auto will set it to your current rank.
    • Last N Games sets the initial zoom level
      • Charts are interactive, try zooming, panning, click in the legend to turn lines on/off, etc
    • There are several averages that respond more or less quickly to recent results.
    • Averages for Gold room and Jade room are kept separately because the player pools are different. Only 4 player is supported.
  • Second chart is a history of your rank and rank points
