My favorite part of playing games is those "Aha!" moments when learning. This is why I've played a lot of different games — it's easier to have those moments when you're still new to the game. But before you can enjoy learning cool concepts, you have to get past the initial wall:

"What the heck am I supposed to be doing?"

My site focuses on helping beginners get over that wall so they can start actually enjoying the discovery process. I've carefully picked the best resources to use as a beginner (plus some going on to intermediate territory), and give a recommended order to use them.
